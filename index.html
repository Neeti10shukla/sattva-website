About SATTVA

SATTVA is an institute dedicated to nurturing creativity and intellect through diverse programs in studies, art, craft, dance, and cultural exploration.

Our Vision

To become a holistic center of learning that empowers students of all ages to discover their passion, sharpen their skills, and embrace their inner artist.

SATTVA blends traditional knowledge with modern education through hands-on activities, workshops, and mentorship. Our programs are designed to promote self-expression, discipline, and cultural values. Whether you're a curious beginner or a passionate learner, SATTVA provides the platform to thrive.

What We Offer

Art

Unleash creativity through drawing, painting, and visual expression.

Dance

Learn classical and contemporary dance forms with rhythm and grace.

Craft

Hands-on activities including origami, clay modeling, and decor art.

Music

Explore vocals, instruments, and music theory in joyful sessions.

Yoga

Balance mind and body with ancient yoga practices and meditation.
